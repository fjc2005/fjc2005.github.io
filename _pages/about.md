I am a third-year undergraduate student at the [VCIP Lab](https://vcip.nankai.edu.cn/), Nankai University, advised by **Prof. Chongyi Li** and **Prof. Chunle Guo**. Since September 2023, I have been enrolled in the **Elite Class of Computer Science** at Nankai University.

My research interests lie in **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and **Vision-Language Models (VLMs)**. I have worked on topics in **multimodal large models** and **Trustworthy AI**. I also serve as a reviewer for the top journal **IEEE TPAMI**.
